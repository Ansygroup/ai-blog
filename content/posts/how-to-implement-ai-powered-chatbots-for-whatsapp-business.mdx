## What Is WhatsApp Business API?

WhatsApp Business API is a paid service that allows businesses to interact with customers programmatically. It enables businesses to send and receive messages, automate tasks, and integrate with CRM systems. To use WhatsApp Business API, businesses need to apply for approval through WhatsApp's parent company, Meta.
