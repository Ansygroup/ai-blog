## Pros and Cons
The following sections highlight the pros and cons of Midjourney and DALL-E 3:

### Midjourney
Midjourney is easy to use and has a fast image generation speed. It supports various image styles and customization options, and integrates with popular platforms like Discord and Slack. However, it has limited image resolution and text [prompt](/posts/how-to-write-better-chatgpt-prompts-2026) length, and batch processing is limited to 10 images at a time.

### DALL-E 3
DALL-E 3 generates high-quality images with high resolution and advanced customization options. It supports [long](/posts/how-to-use-ai-to-summarize-long-documents-2026) text [prompts and](/posts/how-to-use-chatgpt-for-seo) integrates with popular platforms like Adobe Creative Cloud and Microsoft Office. However, it has a steeper learning curve, slower image generation speed, and is more expensive than Midjourney.
