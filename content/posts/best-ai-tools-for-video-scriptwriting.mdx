AI video scriptwriting tools have revolutionized the way we create video content. ## How We Tested
Our team spent 20 hours testing 10 different AI video scriptwriting tools, evaluating their ease of use, template variety, and export quality. We also considered factors like customer support, pricing, and user reviews. Our top picks include Lumen5, Raw Shorts, and GoAnimate (now known as Vyond).

## Lumen5 Review
Lumen5 is a popular AI video scriptwriting tool that offers a range of features, including a free plan with 50 templates, a user-friendly interface, and high-quality exports. It's ideal for social media videos, explainer videos, and blog videos. Lumen5 also integrates with popular platforms like YouTube, Facebook, and Twitter.

## Raw Shorts Review
Raw Shorts is another top contender in the AI video scriptwriting space. It offers a 14-day free trial with unlimited exports, a vast template library, and advanced customization options. Raw Shorts is suitable for businesses, marketers, and entrepreneurs who want to create professional-looking videos without breaking the bank.

## Who Should Use AI Video Scriptwriting Tools?
AI video scriptwriting tools are ideal for marketers, businesses, and entrepreneurs who want to create high-quality video content without extensive video production experience. They're also suitable for social media managers, content creators, and bloggers who need to produce engaging videos quickly.

## Alternatives to AI Video Scriptwriting Tools
If you're not satisfied with AI video scriptwriting tools, you can consider alternative options like traditional video scriptwriting software, such as Final Draft or Celtx, or hire a professional video scriptwriter.
