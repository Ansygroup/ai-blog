### What to Look For
When shopping for a laptop for AI and development, there are several key factors to consider. First and foremost, processing power is crucial, as it determines how quickly you can run complex computations and train machine learning models. Look for laptops with powerful CPUs like the Apple M3 chip or Intel Core i7-13700H, which offer multiple cores and high clock speeds.

Another important consideration is memory and storage, as AI and development applications often require large amounts of data to be processed and stored. A minimum of 16GB of RAM is recommended, and a fast SSD is essential for storing your operating system, applications, and data. Additionally, consider the type of storage, such as DDR5 or LPDDR5x, which offers faster speeds and lower power consumption.

Finally, display quality is also important, as it can affect your productivity and overall user experience. Look for laptops with high-resolution displays, such as 4K or QHD, and features like OLED or IPS, which offer better color accuracy and contrast. The Dell XPS 15 OLED, for example, features a stunning 15.6-inch 3.5K OLED InfinityEdge display that is perfect for developers who need to visualize complex data.
