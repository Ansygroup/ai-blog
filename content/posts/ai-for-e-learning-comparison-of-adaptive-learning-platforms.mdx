## What Is AI for E-Learning?

AI for e-learning refers to the use of artificial [intelligence](/posts/repository-intelligence-github-2026-ai-code-[context](/posts/model-context-protocol-mcp-2026-why-it-matters)))) technologies to enhance the learning experience. Adaptive [learning platforms](/posts/ai-language-learning-platforms), a subset of AI-powered e-learning tools, use machine learning algorithms to adjust the difficulty [level](/posts/best-ai-books-2026) of educational content based on individual [students](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-students-2026)' performance.
