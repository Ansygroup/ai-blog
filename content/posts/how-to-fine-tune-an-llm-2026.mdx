## What Is Fine Tuning an LLM?

Fine tuning an LLM involves adjusting its weights to fit a specific task or dataset. This process can significantly improve the [model](/posts/claude-4-vs-gpt-4o-vs-gemini-1-5-2026)'s performance, making it more accurate and efficient.
