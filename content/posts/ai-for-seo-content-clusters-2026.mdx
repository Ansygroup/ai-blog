## Understanding Content Clusters
Content clusters are groups of related content pieces that target specific keywords and topics. According to a [study](/posts/how-to-use-ai-to-learn-faster-2026) by HubSpot, content clusters can increase [website](/posts/comparison-of-ai-powered-website-builders) traffic by 20%. For example, a fitness [website](/posts/comparison-of-ai-powered-website-builders) could [create](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-social-media-managers-2026) a content cluster around "weight loss" with subtopics like "diet plans," "exercise routines," and "motivation [tips](/posts/how-to-use-ai-for-resume-writing-2026)." **[Ahrefs](/posts/review-of-ai-driven-seo-audit-tools-ahrefs-[semrush](/posts/ai-powered-content-optimization-tools-for-seo)-and-moz)** and **[SEMrush](/posts/ai-seo-audit-tool-comparison)** are popular tools for researching and [creating](/posts/how-to-use-ai-for-creating-interactive-ebooks-and-digital-ma) content clusters.
