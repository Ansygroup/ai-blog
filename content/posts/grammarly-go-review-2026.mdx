## What Is Grammarly Go?

Grammarly Go is an AI-powered writing assistant designed to help with grammar, spell checking, and content creation. It's an evolution of the popular Grammarly tool, with a focus on AI-driven writing suggestions.

## Is Grammarly Go Worth the Investment in 2026?

To determine if Grammarly Go is worth the investment, let's examine its features and pricing. Grammarly Go offers a free plan, as well as several paid tiers, including a Premium plan ($11.66/month) and a [Business](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-small-business-2026) plan ($25/month). ## How Does Grammarly Go Compare to Other AI Writing Assistants?

Grammarly Go competes with other AI writing assistants like ProWritingAid and [Writesonic](/posts/writesonic-review-2026). ProWritingAid offers a more comprehensive writing toolset, including a built-in thesaurus and readability metrics. Writesonic, on the other hand, focuses on AI-powered content creation, with features like article writing and social media post generation.
