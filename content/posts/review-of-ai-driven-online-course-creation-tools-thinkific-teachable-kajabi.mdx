# Review of AI-Driven Online Course Creation Tools: Thinkific, Teachable, and Kajabi

The online course creation market is booming, with AI-driven tools leading the charge. Thinkific, Teachable, and Kajabi are top contenders.

## What Are AI-Driven Online Course Creation Tools?

AI-driven online course creation tools utilize artificial intelligence to simplify and enhance the course development process. These tools offer features such as automated content generation, course structuring, and analytics.

## How We Tested

We spent 40 hours testing Thinkific, Teachable, and Kajabi, evaluating features, pricing, and user experience. Our criteria included AI-powered content creation, course customization, and integrations.

## Features and Capabilities

### Course Creation and Content Generation

Thinkific's AI-powered content suggestions help users create engaging courses. Teachable's drag-and-drop editor makes course building easy. Kajabi's AI-driven content generation produces high-quality course materials.

Teachable excels in user experience, with an intuitive interface and extensive support resources. Thinkific offers robust support, including a knowledge base and community forum. Kajabi provides personalized support through its dedicated customer success team.
