## What Are Frase, Surfer SEO, and Clearscope?

Frase, Surfer SEO, and Clearscope are three popular content optimization tools designed to help you improve your [website](/posts/review-of-ai-based-accessibility-tools-for-websites)'s [search](/news/perplexity-pro-2026-multi-model-routing-review) engine rankings. They offer a range of [features](/news/windsurf-ai-ide-2026-review), including keyword research, content [analysis](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-data-analysis-2026), and optimization recommendations.

We tested each tool over a period of two weeks, evaluating their features, pricing, and performance. Our testing criteria included ease of use, accuracy of optimization recommendations, and overall value.

## Features and Pricing

### Frase

Frase offers a range of features, including:

* AI-powered content optimization
* Keyword research and analysis
* Content scoring and recommendations
* Integration with [Google](/news/gemini-deep-think-2026-google-reasoning-mode-explained) Docs and WordPress

Frase pricing starts at $39/month for the "Basic" plan, which includes 10,000 words per month. The "Pro" plan costs $79/month and includes 30,000 words per month.

### Surfer SEO

Surfer SEO offers:

* Comprehensive SEO audit tool
* On-page optimization recommendations
* Keyword research and analysis
* Integration with Google [Analytics](/posts/ai-predictive-analytics-marketing-tutorial) and Search Console

Surfer SEO pricing starts at $59/month for the "Basic" plan, which includes 100 [pages](/posts/ai-for-landing-pages-2026) per month. The "Pro" plan costs $149/month and includes 500 pages per month.

### Clearscope

Clearscope offers:

* Keyword research and analysis
* Content optimization recommendations
* Semantic SEO and entity-based optimization
* Integration with Google Docs and WordPress

Clearscope pricing starts at $41/month for the "Starter" plan, which includes 100 keywords per month. The "Pro" plan costs $95/month and includes 500 keywords per month.
