## Top AI Video Editing Tools for 2026

### 1. Descript

Descript is a popular AI video editing tool that offers a range of features, including automatic editing, object detection, and color grading. With Descript, you can:

* Edit videos using text-based commands
* Automatically remove filler words and pauses
* Add music and sound effects with a single click

Descript offers a free plan, as well as several paid tiers starting at $15/month.

### 2. Runway

Runway is a powerful AI video editing tool that focuses on advanced visual effects. With Runway, you can:

* Generate realistic visual effects, like explosions and fire
* Create custom 3D models and animations
* Integrate with popular video editing software like Adobe Premiere Pro

Runway offers a free plan, as well as several paid tiers starting at $29/month.

### 3. Adobe Premiere Pro

Adobe Premiere Pro is a professional video editing software that integrates with AI tools like **Adobe Sensei**. With Premiere Pro, you can:

* Automate tasks like color grading and audio ducking
* Use AI-powered editing features like auto-editing and content-aware fill
* Integrate with other Adobe Creative Cloud apps

Premiere Pro offers a free trial, as well as several paid plans starting at $20.99/month.

### 4. Lumen5

Lumen5 is an AI video editing tool that focuses on creating engaging social media videos. With Lumen5, you can:

* Create videos using pre-built templates and AI-powered editing
* Add music and sound effects with a single click
* Integrate with popular social media platforms

Lumen5 offers a free plan, as well as several paid tiers starting at $25/month.

### 5. Wibbitz

Wibbitz is an AI video editing tool that specializes in creating short-form videos. With Wibbitz, you can:

* Create videos using pre-built templates and AI-powered editing
* Add music and sound effects with a single click
* Integrate with popular social media platforms

Wibbitz offers a free plan, as well as several paid tiers starting at $25/month.
