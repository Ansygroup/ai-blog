## Top AI Video Editing Tools [for 2026](/posts/best-smart-home-devices-2026)

### 1. Descript

Descript is a popular AI video editing tool that offers a range of [features](/news/windsurf-ai-ide-2026-review), including automatic editing, object detection, and color grading. With Descript, you can:

* Edit videos using text-based commands
* Automatically remove filler words and pauses
* Add [[music](/posts/suno-vs-udio-2026)](/posts/best-ai-music-generators-2026) and sound effects with a single click

Descript offers a free plan, as well as several paid tiers starting at $15/month.

### 2. Runway

Runway is a [powerful](/posts/best-free-ai-tools-2026) AI video editing tool that focuses on advanced visual effects. With Runway, you can:

* Generate realistic visual effects, like explosions and fire
* Create custom 3D [[[[[models](/posts/microsoft-mai-models-2026-thinking-code-voice-image)](/posts/microsoft-frontier-tuning-2026-custom-ai-models-rl)](/posts/best-laptops-ai-development-2026)](/posts/microsoft-frontier-tuning-2026-custom-ai-models-rl)](/posts/best-laptops-ai-development-2026) and animations
* Integrate with popular video [editing software](/posts/ai-video-editing-software-comparison) like [Adobe](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-designers-2026) Premiere Pro

Runway offers a free plan, as well as several paid tiers starting at $29/month.

### 3. Adobe Premiere Pro

Adobe Premiere Pro is a professional video editing software that integrates with [AI tools](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-translation-2026) like **Adobe Sensei**. With Premiere Pro, you can:

* [Automate tasks](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-ecommerce-2026) like color grading and [audio](/posts/google-flow-veo-3-1-2026-ai-video-production-[native](/posts/gemini-in-chrome-android-2026-native-browser-ai)-audio)-audio) ducking

Lumen5 is an AI video editing tool that focuses on creating engaging [social](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-social-media-managers-2026) media videos. With Lumen5, you can:
