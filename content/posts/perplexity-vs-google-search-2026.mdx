## What Is Perplexity?

Perplexity is an [AI-powered](/posts/review-of-ai-powered-web-scraping-tools) search engine that uses natural language processing (NLP) and machine [learning](/posts/ai-for-e-learning-comparison-of-adaptive-learning-platforms)) to provide direct and conversational answers. Founded in 2022, Perplexity aims to [revolutionize](/posts/ai-tools-for-seo-agencies-2026) the way we interact with search engines.

## What Is Google Search?

Google Search is the most widely-used search engine, with a [market](/posts/vertical-ai-2026-healthcare-legal-3-5-billion-[market](/posts/how-to-use-ai-for-market-research-2026)) share of over 80%. It uses complex algorithms to rank web [pages](/posts/ai-for-landing-pages-2026) and provide relevant results.
