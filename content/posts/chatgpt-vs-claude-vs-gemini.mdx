## [Writing](/posts/how-to-use-ai-for-resume-writing-2026) Test: Claude Wins

We gave each tool the same brief: "[Write](/posts/how-to-write-youtube-scripts-with-ai-2026) a 1,500-word blog post intro about sustainable packaging for a B2B audience."

**ChatGPT** produced clean, structured [copy](/posts/jasper-vs-writesonic-vs-copy-ai-2026) but with telltale "in today's [fast](/posts/best-ssds-storage-ai-2026)-paced world" filler phrases.

**Claude** produced prose that [read](/posts/best-ai-books-2026) like a senior copywriter wrote it. Sentence variety was best; the AI-tell was almost [zero](/posts/best-free-ai-tools-2026).

**Gemini** produced accurate, well-structured [copy](/posts/jasper-vs-writesonic-vs-copy-ai-2026) but slightly more formal/robotic than the others.

**[Winner](/posts/copy-ai-vs-jasper-2026): Claude.** The prose felt human.

## FAQ

### Which is [better](/posts/descript-vs-riverside): ChatGPT or Claude?

### Is Gemini Advanced [worth](/posts/cursor-ai-review-2026-is-it-worth-20-dollars) $20/mo?
Only if you live in [Google](/posts/ai-for-google-ads-optimization) Workspace (Gmail, Docs, Sheets). Otherwise ChatGPT Plus is a [better](/posts/descript-vs-riverside) value.
