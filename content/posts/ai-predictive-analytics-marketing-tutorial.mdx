## FAQ

### What is the difference between predictive analytics and prescriptive analytics?
Predictive analytics forecasts future events, while prescriptive analytics provides recommendations on what actions to take.

Common applications include customer churn prediction, [lead](/posts/ai-linkedin-lead-generation-scripts) scoring, and [personalized](/posts/ai-personalized-email-newsletters) marketing.

### How do I measure the effectiveness of my AI predictive analytics efforts?
