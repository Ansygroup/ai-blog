AI-powered customer support uses artificial intelligence to automate and enhance customer service interactions. This can include chatbots, virtual assistants, and AI-driven analytics tools. * Integrate the chatbot with your existing support infrastructure

## Integrating AI-Powered Ticketing Tools

AI-powered ticketing tools can help streamline your support workflow and reduce response times. When integrating these tools, consider the following:
