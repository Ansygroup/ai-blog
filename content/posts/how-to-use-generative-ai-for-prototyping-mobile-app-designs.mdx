## Overcoming Common Challenges

While generative AI offers numerous benefits, it's not without challenges. Designers may struggle with:

- **Limited control over initial output**: AI-generated designs might not perfectly align with your vision.
- **Potential for generic designs**: AI tools may produce designs that lack a unique brand identity.

To overcome these challenges, consider combining AI-generated designs with human creativity and oversight.

## FAQ

### What are the best AI tools for mobile app design prototyping?

### Can generative AI replace human designers?
No, generative AI is meant to augment and assist human designers, not replace them. AI can handle repetitive tasks and generate ideas quickly, but human creativity and judgment are still essential.

### How do I ensure my AI-generated designs are unique?
To ensure uniqueness, provide specific and detailed input to the AI tool, and consider combining multiple AI-generated elements to create a unique design.

### Can I use AI-generated designs for commercial projects?
Yes, most AI design tools allow commercial use of their generated designs. However, always review the tool's licensing terms and conditions to ensure compliance.
