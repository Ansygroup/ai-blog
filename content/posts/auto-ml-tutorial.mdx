# Training a Custom AI [Model](/posts/claude-4-vs-gpt-4o-vs-gemini-1-5-2026) with No [Code](/posts/best-ai-code-assistants-2026) Using [Google](/posts/google-flow-veo-3-1-2026-ai-video-production-[native](/posts/gemini-in-chrome-android-2026-native-browser-ai)-audio) AutoML
You can train a custom AI model with Google AutoML. No [coding](/posts/best-ai-coding-assistants-2026-comparative-review) is required.

<div class="quick-answer">

## Introduction to No-[Code](/posts/best-ai-code-assistants-2026) AI
No-code AI platforms like [Google](/posts/google-flow-veo-3-1-2026-ai-video-production-[native](/posts/gemini-in-chrome-android-2026-native-browser-ai)-audio) AutoML, H2O AutoML, and BigML are changing the field of artificial intelligence. These tools allow non-technical users to train custom AI [models](/posts/microsoft-frontier-tuning-2026-custom-ai-models-rl) without [writing](/posts/best-ai-writing-tools-for-fiction-2026) code.

## How [Google](/posts/google-flow-veo-3-1-2026-ai-video-production-[native](/posts/gemini-in-chrome-android-2026-native-browser-ai)-audio) AutoML Works
[Google](/posts/google-managed-agents-2026-production-agentic-deployments) AutoML has a simple interface for training custom AI [models](/posts/microsoft-frontier-tuning-2026-custom-ai-models-rl). The platform offers pre-trained models for text, [image](/posts/best-ai-image-generators-2026), and tabular [data](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-data-analysis-2026), making it easy to get started. For example, you can use AutoML to [build](/posts/ai-content-moderation-system-user-generated-[content](/posts/ai-for-seo-content-clusters-2026)) a text classification model to categorize [customer feedback](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-automated-customer-feedback-[analysis](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-data-analysis-2026)) as positive, negative, or neutral.

## Choosing the Right [Model](/posts/claude-4-vs-gpt-4o-vs-gemini-1-5-2026)
When selecting a pre-trained [model](/posts/claude-mythos-2026-anthropic-most-capable-[model](/posts/claude-4-vs-gpt-4o-vs-gemini-1-5-2026)-review) on Google AutoML, consider the type of [data](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-data-analysis-2026) you're working with and the task you want to accomplish. If you're working with [image](/posts/best-ai-image-generators-2026) data, the AutoML Vision [model](/posts/claude-4-vs-gpt-4o-vs-gemini-1-5-2026) may be a good choice. If you're working with text [data](/posts/ai-data-cleaning-tutorial), the AutoML Natural Language model may be a [better](/posts/chatgpt-vs-claude) fit.

## Training a Custom Model
Training a custom model with [Google](/posts/google-flow-veo-3-1-2026-ai-video-production-[native](/posts/gemini-in-chrome-android-2026-native-browser-ai)-audio) AutoML is straightforward. Upload your dataset, configure the hyperparameters, and click the "Train" button. The process takes less than 10 minutes, and you can monitor the training progress in real [time](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-small-business-2026).

## Evaluating Model Performance
After training a custom model, evaluate its performance using metrics like accuracy, precision, and recall. [Google](/posts/google-flow-veo-3-1-2026-ai-video-production-native-audio) AutoML provides evaluation tools, including confusion matrices and ROC curves.

## Deploying a Trained Model
Once you've trained and evaluated a custom model, you can deploy it to platforms like Google Cloud, AWS, and Azure. You can also use the model to make predictions on new [data](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-data-analysis-2026).

## Who Should Use Google AutoML
Google AutoML is ideal for non-technical users who want to train custom AI models without writing code. This includes [business](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-small-business-2026) analysts, data scientists, and [marketers](/posts/prompt-engineering-for-marketers) who want to leverage AI to drive [business](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-small-business-2026) insights.

## Who Should Skip Google AutoML
If you're an experienced data scientist or machine learning engineer, you may prefer more advanced platforms like TensorFlow or PyTorch. If you're working with sensitive data, you may want to consider more secure platforms like Amazon SageMaker.
