## Who Should Use AI Editing Tools
AI editing tools are ideal for podcasters who want to simplify their production workflow and improve the sound quality of their episodes. If you're a professional podcaster or a serious hobbyist, we recommend using **Descript** or **Adobe Audition**. For those on a budget, **Hindenburg Field Recorder** is a great alternative.

If you're just starting out with podcasting or have a very small budget, you may want to consider using free or low-cost editing tools like **Audacity** or **GarageBand**. However, keep in mind that these tools may not offer the same level of features and quality as AI editing tools.
