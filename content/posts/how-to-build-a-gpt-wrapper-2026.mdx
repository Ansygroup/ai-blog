## What Is a GPT Wrapper?

A GPT wrapper is a [software](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-video-editing-2026) application that interacts with a GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) [[[model](/posts/claude-4-vs-gpt-4o-vs-gemini-1-5-2026)](/posts/model-context-protocol-mcp-2026-why-it-matters)](/posts/claude-4-vs-gpt-4o-vs-gemini-1-5-2026), providing a user-friendly interface for users to input [[prompts](/posts/how-to-write-better-chatgpt-prompts-2026) and](/posts/how-to-use-chatgpt-for-seo) receive generated text. GPT wrappers can be used for a variety of applications, such as chatbots, [[[[[[content](/posts/best-ai-writing-tools)](/posts/ai-for-seo-content-clusters-2026)](/posts/frase-vs-surfer-seo-vs-clearscope-2026)](/posts/frase-review-2026)](/posts/best-ai-writing-tools)](/posts/ai-for-seo-content-clusters-2026) [generation](/posts/how-to-use-midjourney-2026), and language [translation](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-translation-2026).
