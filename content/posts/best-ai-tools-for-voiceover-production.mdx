## FAQ
### What is [the best](/posts/the-best-ai-tools-for-content-repurposing) AI voiceover tool [for beginners](/posts/google-ai-studio-2026-best-free-ai-platform)?
Voicely is a great option for beginners, as it offers a [user](/posts/ai-powered-website-testing-tools)-friendly interface and a wide range of voices and languages.

### [How much](/posts/ai-pricing-2026) do AI voiceover tools cost?
The [cost](/posts/best-free-ai-tools-2026) of AI voiceover tools varies depending on the provider and the [features](/posts/windsurf-ai-ide-2026-review) offered. On average, you can expect to pay between $0.05 and $0.10 per minute.

### Can I use AI voiceover tools for commercial purposes?
Yes, most AI voiceover tools offer commercial licenses that allow you to use their voices for commercial purposes, such as advertising and [marketing](/posts/ai-for-affiliate-marketing).

### Are AI voiceover tools [better](/posts/chatgpt-vs-claude) than human voiceovers?
AI voiceover tools have their advantages, but they may not be suitable for every [project](/posts/ai-powered-project-management-tools). Human voiceovers offer a level of emotional expression and nuance that AI voiceovers can't match.

### Can I customize the voices on AI voiceover tools?
Yes, most AI voiceover tools offer some level of customization, such as adjusting the pitch, tone, and speed of the voice.
