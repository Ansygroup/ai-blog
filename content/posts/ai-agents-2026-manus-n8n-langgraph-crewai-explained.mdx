## What Are AI Agents?

AI agents are [software](/posts/ai-video-editing-software-comparison) entities that can perceive their environment, process information, and take actions to achieve specific goals. They are becoming increasingly important in automating complex processes across various industries.

## Manus: No-[Code](/posts/best-ai-code-assistants-2026) AI Agent Builder

Manus offers a no-[code](/posts/best-ai-coding-tools-2026-cursor-windsurf-claude-code-codex) AI [agent](/posts/claude-code-review-2026) builder that allows users to create and deploy AI agents without extensive [coding](/posts/best-ai-coding-assistants-2026-comparative-review) knowledge. ## n8n: [Workflow Automation](/posts/ai-workflow-automation-2026-n8n-vs-zapier-vs-make) Tool

n8n is a workflow automation tool that allows users to create complex [workflows](/posts/ai-automation-with-zapier-2026) using a visual interface. With over 200 nodes and 1000+ integrations, n8n offers extensive customization [options](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-translation-2026). The platform supports a wide range of AI [models](/posts/microsoft-frontier-tuning-2026-custom-ai-models-rl) and services.

## LangGraph: Complex AI Workflows

LangGraph enables the [creation](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-social-media-content-calendar-creation) of complex AI workflows using a graph-based interface. The platform supports a wide range of AI [models](/posts/microsoft-frontier-tuning-2026-custom-ai-models-rl) and services, making it ideal for advanced AI applications. LangGraph offers a high degree of customization.

## CrewAI: Autonomous AI Agents

CrewAI specializes in autonomous AI agents for various industries, including healthcare, finance, and [customer](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-automated-customer-feedback-[analysis](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-data-analysis-2026)) service. The platform offers a range of pre-built agents and allows users to create custom agents. CrewAI focuses on delivering high accuracy and reliability.
