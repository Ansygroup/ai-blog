## Understanding Midjourney 2026 Parameters

Midjourney 2026 parameters allow you to [fine](/posts/how-to-fine-tune-an-llm-2026)-tune your image generation process. Parameters like `--ar` (aspect ratio), `--q` (quality), and `--st` (style) can significantly impact the output. For example, using `--ar 16:9` will generate an image in widescreen format, while `--q 2` will produce a higher-quality image at the [cost](/posts/best-free-ai-tools-2026) of longer processing times.
