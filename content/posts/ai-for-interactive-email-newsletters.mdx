## Creating Interactive [Content](/posts/ai-for-seo-content-clusters-2026) with AI
To create interactive [content](/posts/ai-powered-content-generation-tools) with AI, you'll need to use tools like SurveyMonkey or Typeform to add polls, [quizzes](/posts/how-to-use-ai-for-creating-interactive-quizzes-and-surveys), and other engaging elements to your emails. For example, you can use SurveyMonkey to create a poll that asks subscribers about their interests, then use [the data](/posts/surfer-seo-review-2026) to personalize future emails. You can also use Typeform to create [interactive quizzes](/posts/how-to-use-ai-for-creating-interactive-quizzes-and-surveys) that help subscribers discover new products or [services](/posts/best-ai-tools-for-transcription-services).
