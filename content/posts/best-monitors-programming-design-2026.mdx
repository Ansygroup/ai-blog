### What to Look For
When shopping for the best monitors for programming and design, there are several key factors to consider. The display's resolution, refresh rate, and response time are crucial for a smooth and seamless viewing experience. A 4K UHD display with a high refresh rate, such as 160Hz or 175Hz, and a fast response time, such as 1ms or 0.03ms, is ideal for tasks that require fast rendering and minimal lag.
